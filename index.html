About Pestana CR7 Lisboa

Located in the heart of Lisbon, Pestana CR7 Lisboa combines contemporary design with authentic Portuguese charm to create a truly distinctive stay. Inspired by the legendary footballer Cristiano Ronaldo, the hotel reflects a dynamic and modern spirit, blending sleek interiors with subtle tributes to the world of football. Its prime location places guests within walking distance of Lisbon’s historic landmarks, vibrant nightlife, and iconic riverfront, making it an ideal base for both leisure and business travelers seeking comfort, style, and a memorable experience.

Guests can relax in spacious, thoughtfully designed rooms that offer modern amenities and a welcoming atmosphere. The hotel features a rooftop pool where visitors can unwind while enjoying breathtaking panoramic views of the city skyline. A fully equipped fitness center allows guests to maintain their workout routines throughout their stay. In addition, the hotel’s trendy on-site restaurant serves a selection of delicious local and international dishes, providing a perfect setting to savor Lisbon’s rich culinary flavors in a stylish and relaxed environment.
